The Comparison of total profits in four kinds of drinks between twenty states in the US.

The treemap displays a comparison of total profits in four kinds of drinks between twenty states in the US. The drinks include Coffee, Tea, Herbal Tea and Espresso, which have the attributes of profits, sales, detailed types and dates. The treemap focus on performance on the attribute of profits based on each categery and each state. The interactions of hover and tooltips have been created.
